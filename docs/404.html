Hi, I’m Allen.

I’m a software developer, but I’ve been at times a composer, cruise ship musician and artist. I love making beautiful things, teaching others and figuring out how things work. My specialty is web development, both front-end and back-end, but I also have experience with native iOS, Android and Mac development, as well as cross-platform mobile development.
